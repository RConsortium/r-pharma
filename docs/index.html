About

This conference will showcase the current use of R that is helping to drive biomedical research, drug discovery; development, and clinical initiatives. The R / Pharma Conference is an opportunity for Pharmaceutical professionals to learn new techniques, best practices, success stories, tools, practical solutions, network with peers, and learn further about the use of R in the pharmaceutical science industry. A central location for resources and best practices related to the activities of using R in the drug development process will be available. A long term goal will be documentation on pharma activities pertaining to evolving industry standards (CDISC SDTM, SEND, ADaM) related to clinical, operational and safety data.
