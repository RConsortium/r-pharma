Companies attending R/Pharma are able to provide support to cover travel and accomodation for several conference attendees that are currently enrolled as students, or working for a non-profit. Scholarships will be given out on a rolling basis, so early submissions are encouraged.

How will attending the conference impact you? What important problems will you be able to tackle that you couldn’t before? You will learn the most if you already have some experience with R, so show us what you’ve achieved so far.

How will you share your knowledge with others? We can’t help everyone, so we’re particularly interested in helping those who will go back to their communities and spread the love. Show us how you’ve shared your code, skills and knowledge in the past and tell us what you plan to do in the future.
